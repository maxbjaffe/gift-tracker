## üé® Visual & UX Enhancements

### 1. Gift Wish List Sharing
**What:** Generate a shareable link for recipients to see their gift ideas
**Why:** Let people hint at what they want without being too direct
**Features:**
- Public/private toggle per recipient
- Shareable URL (e.g., giftstash.app/wishlist/sarah-2024)
- Mark gifts as "claimed" anonymously
- Prevent recipient from seeing who's buying what

### 2. Price Drop Alerts
**What:** Monitor gift prices and notify when they go on sale
**Why:** Save money on gifts you're planning to buy
**Features:**
- Track price history
- Set price drop threshold (e.g., notify if 20% off)
- Email/push notifications
- Integration with price tracking APIs (Honey, CamelCamelCamel)

### 3. Gift Occasion Reminders
**What:** Smart notifications before birthdays, holidays, etc.
**Why:** Never forget an important gift-giving occasion
**Features:**
- Calendar integration
- Customizable reminder windows (1 week, 2 weeks before)
- "Suggested gifts" based on recipient interests
- Countdown widgets

### 4. Gift Wrapping Tracker
**What:** Track which gifts are wrapped and where they're hidden
**Why:** Stay organized during busy gift seasons
**Features:**
- "Wrapped" status (we have this!)
- "Hidden location" field (e.g., "hall closet")
- "Wrapping paper used" tracking
- Print gift tags

### 5. Budget Planning
**What:** Set budgets per recipient or occasion
**Why:** Keep gift spending under control
**Features:**
- Monthly/annual budget limits
- Budget vs. actual spending charts
- Alerts when approaching budget limit
- Recommended budget based on relationship
